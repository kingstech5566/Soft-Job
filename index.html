Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar.
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon.
  • 6 medium-sezied apples, peeled and sliced.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375oF (190oC).
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamone mixture.
